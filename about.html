About FaithAssist

FaithAssist was created to help people find biblical guidance, prayer, and practical support — completely free, without login, without judgment.

Our Mission

We believe everyone deserves access to God's wisdom, regardless of their background or financial situation. Our tools are designed to be simple, helpful, and respectful.

What We Believe

  • The Bible is God's Word and offers guidance for every area of life.
  • Prayer connects us with God and brings peace.
  • Everyone struggles, and faith provides hope and strength.
  • We are called to love, not judge.

Our Team

FaithAssist is run by a small team of Christian volunteers who believe in making faith resources accessible to all.
