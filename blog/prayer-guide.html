How to Pray: A Simple Biblical Guide When You Don't Know What to Say

📅 January 2025 | ✝️ 6 min read | 📖 Learning to pray

"I don't know how to pray." "My prayers feel so weak." "I get distracted." If you've felt this way, you're not alone. Even the disciples asked Jesus, "Lord, teach us to pray" (Luke 11:1).

The Lord's Prayer: Jesus' Model

📖 Matthew 6:9-13
"Our Father in heaven, hallowed be your name, your kingdom come, your will be done, on earth as it is in heaven. Give us today our daily bread. And forgive us our debts, as we also have forgiven our debtors. And lead us not into temptation, but deliver us from the evil one."

The ACTS Method (Easy to Remember)

  • A - Adoration: Praise God for who He is. "Lord, You are good, faithful, powerful..."
  • C - Confession: Honestly admit your sins. "Forgive me for..."
  • T - Thanksgiving: Thank God for what He's done. "Thank You for..."
  • S - Supplication: Ask for your needs and others'. "Please help with..."

Tips for Better Prayer

  1. Pray Scripture – Turn Bible verses into prayers.
  2. Pray out loud – It helps focus your mind.
  3. Pray at a set time – Build a habit (morning, mealtime, bedtime).
  4. Pray short prayers – They don't have to be long to be powerful.
  5. Pray with others – Jesus promised to be where two or three gather.
🙏 Simple Daily Prayer Template

"Good morning, Lord. Thank You for this day. I confess that I need You. Please help me to [specific need]. Thank You for [one thing you're grateful for]. I pray for [someone else's need]. In Jesus' name, Amen."
