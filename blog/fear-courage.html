Overcoming Fear

Bible verses for courage

📖 Isaiah 41:10
"So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you."
📖 2 Timothy 1:7
"For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind."
📖 Joshua 1:9
"Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid... for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go."

Practical Steps to Overcome Fear

  1. Name the fear – write it down
  2. Speak Scripture aloud
  3. Take one small step
  4. Share with someone
🙏 A Prayer for Courage

"Lord, I'm afraid. But I choose to trust You more than my fear. Give me courage to take the next step. Remind me that You are with me. Amen."
