5 Prayers for Marriage

Powerful prayers for restoration and connection

1. Prayer for Communication

"Lord, teach us to communicate with kindness. Help me to listen before I speak. Remove defensiveness from our conversations. Let our words be full of grace. Amen."

2. Prayer for Trust

"Father, trust has been broken. Heal our marriage. Give the one who hurt true repentance. Give the one hurt supernatural ability to forgive. Restore what the enemy has stolen. Amen."

3. Prayer for Emotional Intimacy

"God, we feel like roommates instead of lovers. Restore our emotional connection. Help us to be vulnerable and to prioritize our relationship. Amen."

4. Prayer for Conflict Resolution

"Lord, our fights are destructive. Help us to fight fair – no name-calling, no silent treatment. Help us apologize quickly and forgive completely. Amen."

5. Prayer for Spiritual Unity

"Father, unite us spiritually. Help us to pray together and read Your Word together. Make our marriage a testimony of Your faithfulness. Amen."
