How to Make Big Decisions: Biblical Guidance

📅 January 2025 | ✝️ 9 min read

Should I take this job? Marry this person? Move to this city? Big decisions are stressful because the stakes are high. But God promises wisdom to those who ask.

📖 James 1:5
"If any of you lacks wisdom, you should ask God, who gives generously to all without finding fault, and it will be given to you."
God doesn't mock you for not knowing. He GIVES wisdom generously. Ask!

5 Biblical Principles for Decision-Making

  1. Pray first, not last – Bring the decision to God before you've already decided.
  2. Seek Scripture – Does your choice align with biblical values? If it violates God's Word, it's not His will.
  3. Get godly counsel – Proverbs 15:22 says plans fail without counsel. Ask wise, mature Christians.
  4. Check your motives – Are you making this decision out of fear, greed, or pride? Or love and wisdom?
  5. Look for open/closed doors – Sometimes God closes doors to redirect you. Pay attention.
📖 Proverbs 3:5-6
"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding. In all your ways acknowledge him, and he will make your paths straight."
Don't rely on your limited understanding. Acknowledge God in the process, and He will guide.
🙏 Prayer for Decision-Making

"Lord, I don't know what to do. I'm afraid of making the wrong choice. Give me wisdom. Close the wrong doors and open the right ones. Help me to trust Your guidance even when I can't see the whole path. I choose to follow You, even if it's scary. Amen."
