Finding Financial Peace

What the Bible says about money

Money stress is a leading cause of anxiety and marriage conflict. The Bible has over 2,000 verses about finances – God cares about your money because He cares about YOU.

📖 Philippians 4:19
"And my God will meet all your needs according to the riches of his glory in Christ Jesus."
Not "some needs" – ALL your needs. Your bank account isn't your source; God is.
📖 Matthew 6:31-33
"Seek first his kingdom and his righteousness, and all these things will be given to you as well."
Prioritize God. When you seek Him first, He handles the rest.
📖 Proverbs 22:7
"The borrower is slave to the lender."
Debt creates a form of slavery. While not all debt is sin, the Bible warns against being controlled by lenders.
📖 Malachi 3:10
"Test me in this," says the Lord, "and see if I will not throw open the floodgates of heaven."
God invites us to TEST Him in giving. Generosity opens supernatural provision.
📖 1 Timothy 6:10
"The love of money is a root of all kinds of evil."
Note: It's the LOVE of money, not money itself. Your heart's attachment matters.
🙏 A Prayer for Financial Peace

"Lord, I confess that I've worried about money. You own everything. Help me to be a faithful steward, a cheerful giver, and a wise saver. I trust You, not my paycheck. Amen."
