Trusting God When Life Doesn't Make Sense

📅 January 2025 | ✝️ 7 min read | 📖 Faith in hard times

Trusting God is easy when life is good. But when tragedy strikes, prayers seem unanswered, and the future looks dark – trust becomes a battle.

The Ultimate Trust Verse

📖 Proverbs 3:5-6
"Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding. In all your ways acknowledge him, and he will make your paths straight."
Two commands: (1) Trust God completely, not partially. (2) Don't rely on your own understanding – because your understanding is limited. You can't see the whole picture.
📖 Isaiah 55:8-9
"For my thoughts are not your thoughts, neither are your ways my ways, declares the Lord. As the heavens are higher than the earth, so are my ways higher than your ways and my thoughts than your thoughts."
God's perspective is infinitely bigger than yours. What looks like a dead end to you might be a detour to something better.

Stories of Trust in the Bible

  • Abraham – Trusted God to provide a son, then trusted Him when asked to sacrifice that son.
  • Joseph – Sold into slavery, falsely imprisoned, but saw God's plan years later.
  • Job – Lost everything, but said, "Though He slay me, yet will I trust Him."
🙏 A Prayer for Trust

"Lord, I don't understand what's happening. This isn't what I planned or prayed for. But I choose to trust You – not because I see the way, but because I know You. Help me to lean not on my own understanding. Give me peace in the waiting. I believe that You are working, even when I can't see it. Amen."
