10 Bible Verses for Anxiety and Worry

📅 January 2025 | ✝️ 8 min read | 📖 Scripture for peace

Anxiety is one of the most common struggles Christians face today. According to the Anxiety & Depression Association of America, over 40 million adults suffer from anxiety disorders. But even if you don't have a clinical diagnosis, worry and fear can feel overwhelming.

The good news? God's Word speaks directly to our anxious hearts. These 10 Bible verses for anxiety will help you find peace, remember God's presence, and release your worries to Him.

📖 In this article: 1. Philippians 4:6-7 - Pray Instead of Worry 2. 1 Peter 5:7 - Cast Your Anxiety on Him 3. Isaiah 41:10 - Do Not Fear, I Am With You 4. Matthew 6:34 - Don't Worry About Tomorrow 5. John 14:27 - My Peace I Give You 6. Psalm 34:4 - I Sought the Lord, He Answered 7. Romans 8:28 - All Things Work for Good 8. 2 Timothy 1:7 - Spirit of Power, Not Fear 9. Psalm 55:22 - Cast Your Burdens on the Lord 10. Joshua 1:9 - Be Strong and Courageous
📖 1. Philippians 4:6-7
"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God. And the peace of God, which transcends all understanding, will guard your hearts and your minds in Christ Jesus."
Paul doesn't just say "stop worrying" – he gives a solution: pray about everything. When you bring your fears to God, He replaces them with supernatural peace that doesn't make logical sense. That's the beauty of it!
📖 2. 1 Peter 5:7
"Cast all your anxiety on him because he cares for you."
The word "cast" means to throw onto something. Imagine physically throwing your worries onto God's shoulders. He can handle them – and He actually cares about what you're going through.
📖 3. Isaiah 41:10
"So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand."
This verse contains five powerful promises: Don't fear (command), I am with you (presence), don't be dismayed (comfort), I am your God (relationship), I will strengthen you (power). God isn't asking you to be strong alone.
📖 4. Matthew 6:34
"Therefore do not worry about tomorrow, for tomorrow will worry about itself. Each day has enough trouble of its own."
Jesus gives practical wisdom: focus on today. Most of what we worry about never happens. And even if it does, worrying today won't help you face it tomorrow. Give each day its own attention.
📖 5. John 14:27
"Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you. I do not give to you as the world gives. Do not let your hearts be troubled and do not be afraid."
The world's peace depends on circumstances (vacation, money, health). Jesus' peace exists regardless of circumstances. It's a gift – you just have to receive it.
📖 6. Psalm 34:4
"I sought the Lord, and he answered me; he delivered me from all my fears."
David wrote this after a terrifying experience. His testimony is simple: when he sought God, God answered. Not "maybe" or "later" – He answered. Try it today.
📖 7. Romans 8:28
"And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him, who have been called according to his purpose."
Not "some things" or "good things" – ALL things. Your current struggle isn't wasted. God is weaving it into something good, even if you can't see it yet.
📖 8. 2 Timothy 1:7
"For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind."
Fear doesn't come from God. If you're feeling overwhelmed by fear, recognize its source – and reject it. God offers power (to act), love (to connect), and sound mind (clarity).
📖 9. Psalm 55:22
"Cast your cares on the Lord and he will sustain you; he will never let the righteous be shaken."
When you give God your burdens, He gives you stability. You won't be "shaken" by circumstances because your foundation is Him.
📖 10. Joshua 1:9
"Have I not commanded you? Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go."
God commands courage – meaning it's a choice, not a feeling. You can act brave even when you feel scared, because God promises His presence everywhere you go.
🙏 A Prayer for Anxiety

"Lord, I bring my anxious thoughts to You right now. I choose to believe Your Word over my feelings. Replace my fear with Your perfect peace. Help me to pray instead of worry, and to trust that You are working all things for good. Thank You that I don't have to be strong alone – You are with me. In Jesus' name, Amen."
