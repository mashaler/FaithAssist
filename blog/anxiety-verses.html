10 Bible Verses for Anxiety

Find peace through Scripture when worry overwhelms you

Anxiety affects millions of people. The good news? God's Word speaks directly to our anxious hearts. These 10 verses will help you find peace.

📖 Philippians 4:6-7
"Do not be anxious about anything, but in every situation, by prayer and petition, with thanksgiving, present your requests to God."
Paul doesn't just say "stop worrying" – he gives a solution: pray about everything. When you bring your fears to God, He replaces them with supernatural peace.
📖 1 Peter 5:7
"Cast all your anxiety on him because he cares for you."
The word "cast" means to throw onto something. Imagine physically throwing your worries onto God's shoulders. He can handle them.
📖 Isaiah 41:10
"So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you."
Five promises in one verse: Don't fear, I'm with you, I'm your God, I'll strengthen you, I'll help you.
📖 Matthew 6:34
"Therefore do not worry about tomorrow, for tomorrow will worry about itself. Each day has enough trouble of its own."
Jesus gives practical wisdom: focus on today. Most of what we worry about never happens.
📖 John 14:27
"Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you. I do not give to you as the world gives."
The world's peace depends on circumstances. Jesus' peace exists regardless of circumstances.
📖 Psalm 34:4
"I sought the Lord, and he answered me; he delivered me from all my fears."
David's testimony: when he sought God, God answered. Try it today.
📖 Romans 8:28
"And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love him."
Not "some things" – ALL things. Your current struggle isn't wasted. God is weaving it into something good.
📖 2 Timothy 1:7
"For God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power and of love and of a sound mind."
Fear doesn't come from God. He offers power, love, and clarity.
📖 Psalm 55:22
"Cast your cares on the Lord and he will sustain you; he will never let the righteous be shaken."
When you give God your burdens, He gives you stability.
📖 Joshua 1:9
"Be strong and courageous. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged, for the Lord your God will be with you wherever you go."
God commands courage – meaning it's a choice, not a feeling.
🙏 A Prayer for Anxiety

"Lord, I bring my anxious thoughts to You right now. Replace my fear with Your perfect peace. Help me to pray instead of worry. Thank You that I don't have to be strong alone. Amen."
