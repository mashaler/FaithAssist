Comfort for Grief

Bible verses for loss and sorrow

📖 Psalm 34:18
"The Lord is close to the brokenhearted and saves those who are crushed in spirit."
Not far away – CLOSE. Right there in your pain.
📖 Matthew 5:4
"Blessed are those who mourn, for they will be comforted."
Mourning is blessed because God promises comfort.
📖 Revelation 21:4
"He will wipe every tear from their eyes. There will be no more death or mourning or crying or pain."
One day, grief will end. No more tears. No more goodbyes.
📖 John 11:35
"Jesus wept."
The shortest verse shows us that grief is holy. You don't have to "be strong."
🙏 A Prayer for Grief

"Father, my heart is broken. Hold me close. Help me to grieve without guilt and to cry without shame. Give me hope that I will see them again in heaven. Amen."
