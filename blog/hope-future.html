Hope for the Future

Bible promises when you feel hopeless

📖 Jeremiah 29:11
"For I know the plans I have for you, declares the Lord, plans to prosper you and not to harm you, plans to give you hope and a future."
God has plans. Good plans. Hope-filled plans. Even if you can't see them yet.
📖 Romans 15:13
"May the God of hope fill you with all joy and peace as you trust in him, so that you may overflow with hope."
📖 Hebrews 11:1
"Faith is confidence in what we hope for and assurance about what we do not see."
📖 Revelation 21:4
"He will wipe every tear from their eyes. There will be no more death or mourning or crying or pain."
🙏 A Prayer for Hope

"Lord, I feel hopeless. The future looks dark. But I know You are the God of hope. Fill me with hope by Your Spirit. Remind me that this season is not the end of my story. Amen."
