7 Prayers for Parenting

Patience, wisdom, and grace for every parent

1. Prayer for Patience

"Lord, give me patience. When my child pushes my buttons, help me respond with grace, not anger. Let me model self-control. Amen."

2. Prayer for Wisdom

"Father, I don't know how to handle this. Give me wisdom to know when to be firm and when to be gentle. Show me what my child really needs. Amen."

3. Prayer for Your Child's Future

"God, protect my child. Surround them with godly friends. Give them a heart that seeks You. Guide their steps even when I can't be there. Amen."

4. Prayer for Peace at Home

"Lord, bring peace to our home. Replace yelling with calm voices. Replace sibling rivalry with friendship. Let our home be a refuge. Amen."
📖 Proverbs 22:6
"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it."
📖 Ephesians 6:4
"Do not exasperate your children; instead, bring them up in the training and instruction of the Lord."
