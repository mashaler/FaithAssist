How to Forgive

Biblical steps to freedom when forgiveness feels impossible

Forgiveness is one of the hardest commands Jesus gave us. But unforgiveness hurts YOU more than the person who wronged you.

📖 Matthew 6:14-15
"If you forgive other people when they sin against you, your heavenly Father will also forgive you."
Jesus connects our forgiveness of others directly to God's forgiveness of us.
📖 Colossians 3:13
"Forgive as the Lord forgave you."
The standard: forgive the way God forgave you – completely.
📖 Ephesians 4:31-32
"Get rid of all bitterness, rage and anger... Be kind and compassionate, forgiving each other."
Bitterness is poison. Replace it with kindness and compassion.
📖 Matthew 18:21-22
"I tell you, not seven times, but seventy-seven times."
Unlimited forgiveness. Not because the person deserves it, but because you need to let go.

What Forgiveness IS and IS NOT

  • Forgiveness is NOT pretending it didn't hurt
  • Forgiveness is NOT automatic reconciliation
  • Forgiveness IS releasing the debt – choosing not to punish
  • Forgiveness IS a decision, not a feeling
🙏 A Prayer for Forgiveness

"Father, I choose to forgive [name]. I release them from the debt they owe me. Help me to truly mean this. Thank You for forgiving me when I didn't deserve it. Amen."
