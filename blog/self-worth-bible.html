Your Identity in Christ: What God Says About You

📅 January 2025 | ✝️ 6 min read | 📖 Your true identity

Low self-worth is a silent epidemic. Many of us believe lies: "I'm not good enough," "I'm a failure," "No one really loves me." But God's Word speaks a different truth over you.

What God Says About You

📖 Psalm 139:14
"I praise you because I am fearfully and wonderfully made; your works are wonderful, I know that full well."
You aren't an accident. You were "fearfully" (with awe) and "wonderfully" (with skill) made by God Himself.
📖 Ephesians 2:10
"For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do."
The word "handiwork" in Greek is "poiema" – where we get "poem." You are God's poem. His masterpiece.
📖 Genesis 1:27
"So God created mankind in his own image, in the image of God he created them; male and female he created them."
Your worth isn't based on performance – it's based on being made in God's image. That never changes.
📖 Romans 5:8
"But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us."
God loved you at your worst. Not after you cleaned up. While you were still a mess. That's real love.

Stop Comparing Yourself

Comparison is a thief of joy. When you compare your behind-the-scenes with everyone else's highlight reel, you'll always feel inadequate. God didn't create you to be someone else. He created you to be YOU.

🙏 A Prayer for Self-Worth

"Father, I confess that I've believed lies about my worth. I've compared myself to others and felt like I don't measure up. But today I choose to believe what You say about me: I am fearfully and wonderfully made. I am Your handiwork. I am loved unconditionally. Help me to see myself the way You see me. In Jesus' name, Amen."
